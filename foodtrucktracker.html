Food Truck Tracker

Food Truck Tracker was created to let foodies find their favorite food trucks no matter where they are located. Users can sign up to find local food trucks, view their menus and leave reviews. Truck owners can list their trucks, add their menus, and update the trucks locations. This project was built using HTML, CSS, React.js, Redux, Styled Components, and Node.js. Food Truck tracker also uses the Google Maps, Places, and Geolocation APIs.
