React Tetris

React Tetris is a recreation of the classic russian puzzle video game Tetris. Try to fit the falling blocks together by moving and rotating the blocks to complete full lines, clearing the line and increasing your score. As you clear move lines, the rate that the blocks fall begins to increase. How many lines can you clear? This project uses HTML, CSS, and React.js.
